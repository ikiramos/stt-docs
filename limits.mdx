## Allowance

Connecting your AI is included with **every paid SearchTheTrend plan**.

| | Included |
|---|---|
| Assistant requests | **10,000 per month** |
| Burst | Up to 30 requests per minute |
| Read tools | Unlimited within the monthly allowance |

The monthly counter resets on the 1st. Free accounts can't connect an assistant — [upgrade](https://searchthetrend.com/settings/mcp) to get a key or connector.

## Credits

Read tools (search, get, history) don't cost credits — only the monthly request allowance applies.

**Brand scans cost credits.** Launching a live scan of a brand not yet tracked costs the same as an in-app brand request. If a scan fails, the credits are refunded automatically. Buy more credits anytime from [Settings → Manage subscription → Buy credits](https://searchthetrend.com/brand?buy_credits=1).

## The giant-brand policy

Scans of large, consolidated advertisers — think Nike, Zara, Gymshark — are **refused without charge**. They run thousands of pure branding ads with no product-discovery value, so the scanner declines and refunds you, then points you at the fastest-growing emerging brands in the same niche instead.

## Safety

Every tool except brand scans is **read-only** — your assistant can look but never change your account or data. Credit-spending actions always ask for your confirmation first.
