title: "claude.ai & Claude Desktop"
description: "One-click connector, no API key."
---

Works on claude.ai (web) and the Claude desktop app. Requires a Claude plan with custom connectors (Pro or above) and a **paid SearchTheTrend plan**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open connector settings">
    In Claude: **Settings → Connectors → Add custom connector**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste the SearchTheTrend URL">
    ```text
    https://searchthetrend.com/api/mcp
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect and sign in">
    Click **Connect**. A SearchTheTrend login opens: sign in with your account and approve. You'll see the permissions screen once, and access is remembered afterwards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try it">
    Start a new chat and ask:
    > *"Reconstruct the selling strategy of [any store domain you're curious about]."*
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Claude loads the SearchTheTrend tools it needs per question. If a brand question doesn't pick up the connector, mention it explicitly: *"using SearchTheTrend, analyze…"*
</Tip>

## Scheduled tasks

Claude's scheduled tasks turn any prompt into a recurring brief. Try scheduling:

> *"Every morning, check the trending products in my niche targeting Germany and summarize the top 3 with who advertises them and the angle they use."*
